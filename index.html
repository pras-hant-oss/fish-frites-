About Us

A Taste of Scotland on the Royal Mile

Bringing the legendary flavours of Anstruther's famous fish and chips to the heart of Edinburgh's historic Royal Mile. Our food truck serves up freshly prepared, made-to-order Scottish classics using the finest locally sourced ingredients.

From our signature Fresh Scottish Haddock to our traditional Haggis & Chips, every dish is crafted with passion and served with a side of Scottish hospitality. We use MSC-certified sustainable seafood and offer gluten-free options for many of our menu items.
